Source Details
Ethical Issues in 3D Printing - NCBI Author: National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI)
Summary: This article explores ethical concerns in 3D printing, particularly regarding intellectual property, bioprinting, and sustainability. It raises questions about the ownership of 3D-printed designs, the impact of printing human organs, and the environmental effects of plastic waste generated by 3D printing.
Legal and Ethical Considerations in 3D Printing - Ian Coll McEachern Author: Ian Coll McEachern
Summary: This article examines the legal risks of 3D printing, including copyright infringement, counterfeiting, and the production of unregulated firearms. It discusses the challenges governments face in enforcing intellectual property laws and controlling the spread of 3D-printed weapons.
